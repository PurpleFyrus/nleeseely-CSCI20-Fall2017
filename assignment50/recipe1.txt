recipe:
hamburger helper
ingredients:
4
1
pound(s) of hamburger
1
pound(s) of rice
1
cup(s) of milk
2
cup(s) of water
instructions:
4
1. Brown beef in 10- or 12-inch skillet. Remove from heat, drain.
2. Stir in Sauce Mix and hot water until dissolved. Stir in 1 cup milk and Rice. Return to heat, heat to boiling.
3. Reduce heat. Cover, simmer about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until rice is tender. Meanwhile, stir 1/3 cup milk and Topping Mix in bowl.
4. Remove from heat. Sprinkle with Corn Chips. Add topping. Refridgerate leftovers.
